### Slice 2 — About ✅

**Navigate:** Profile page → scroll to About → click pencil icon

**Paste:**
```
Most product teams fail not because individuals are incompetent, but because the phases of the product cycle are owned by people who cannot see beyond their own phase.

The engineer builds what was specified. The designer specifies what was researched. The marketer launches what neither of them understands commercially. No one holds the whole picture.

I have spent fifteen years learning to hold the whole cycle — deliberately, one phase at a time.

IBM gave me the foundation. Nine years across two distinct roles: Fullstack Engineer building production systems at scale (Watson IoT Platform, Carbon Design System, the On/Off Boarding experience used by 50%+ of IBM's global contractor base), and Design Thinking Lead at IBM Studios São Paulo — where I ran the "Code is Design" program that activated over 100 engineers and designers in Design Thinking, Agile, and DevOps. What IBM taught me: the gap between what engineers build and what users need is not a technical problem. It is a perception problem.

OutSystems sharpened the product instinct. Three years building IDE features used by thousands of developers daily — the Edit Data experience, the 5-minute Onboarding flow, multi-platform web components for Windows and Mac. Building for developers as users sets an unusually high bar: they can see every seam in your product.

Since 2023, I have been closing the loop. I left OutSystems to answer one question: can a single person — with the right tools — take a product from a user problem to revenue? The answer is yes, if you own every phase.

I built a real estate due diligence platform for the Portuguese market. A career coaching platform that generates AI-powered flight plans through a multi-prompt n8n pipeline. In 2025 I went deep on digital product launch — studying Jeff Walker's Product Launch Formula and Leandro Ladeira's VTSD methodology (the perpetual sales system whose lineage traces to Ryan Deiss, Russell Brunson, Eugene Schwartz, and Dan Kennedy). Then I automated it: AI agent pipelines that execute market research, product positioning, ad creative generation, and email sequences — with human approval at each stage.

Product Launch as Code. That is where I am now.

I am open to roles and collaborations where the full cycle matters — where someone who can conceive, build, launch, and automate is worth more than four people who each hold one phase.

Stack: React · TypeScript · Next.js · Node.js · Cloudflare Workers · PostgreSQL · n8n · Claude Code · Meta Ads · Google Ads · IBM Design Thinking · PLF · VTSD

#ProductEngineer #AIEngineering #DesignThinking #ProductLaunch #AgenticDevelopment
```
